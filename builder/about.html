About us



Over the past few years K. Anand Constuction Pvt. Ltd., (Builders & Developers), have built up a niche for themselves in the field of ‘Building & Construction’, known for executing residential as well as commercial projects in and around Mumbai Suburbs in their own inimitable style with agile skills and styles and in tune with the rapidly changing times, the Company has accomplished all the given task in its short but promising history and is rearing to go ahead will full commitment, force, and confidence to confront more and more milestones in the times to come.
Mr. Awadhesh Anand a competent person, is the dynamic Director of the company and is backed with a solid, vast and varied experience of more than 10 years experience in the field of construction, and is all out to capture the horizon of ‘redevelopment with its inherent challenges. The Company is evolved with a few basic mottos viz Quality at all cost, Excellent Client Relationship, a strong resolution to build up and enhance ‘Growth’ at all levels, keeping focus of the company on completing the given task with the scheduled time frame, zero complaints from clients, and enhancing customer satisfaction. The Company has built up a competent and skilled organizational structure, capable of effectively meeting all challenges at all times. We have quality as our motto – that is Quality at its very best with absolutely no compromise whatsoever. The Company and its team is moving ahead with the challenges and with a firm resolve to establish across the frontier – that the Company is in the core of REDEVELOPMENT. The Company is at its best to create a bond of trust with their clients. The dictum of ‘Quality’ is firmly embedded in our projects and a growing list of gratified clients lends support to it. The Company offers their best because it always tries to get the best. The Company strictly adheres to lending importance to the client’s voice, ethics in business transaction, continuous up gradation, sincere abidance to project completion deadlines, quality being the core matter in all dealings there is no place for compromise, and the asset of the company being its work force.

‘IMAGINATION, IMPEMENTATION, AND IMPROVISATION ABOVE ALL INSTIGATE THE COMPANY AT ALL TIMES AND AT ALL LEVELS’ TO PRODUCE THE BEST’.
