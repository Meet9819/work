We are Best in Redevelopment

What is Redevelopment ?
Redevlopment is the process of demolishing existing old dilapidated society building and reconstructing it by appointing a good developer who can construct and handover new flats to the society members free to cost with better modern day amenties and make profit by utizing balance plot potential by constructing additional flats and shops as per approval from the concerned development authorities.
Why Redevelopment ?
The Old Society building were built over a period on 3-4 decades & are in very bad shape. Infrastructure facilities for electricity, water & sewage are overloaded needs immediate replacement. Socities need huge funds to undertake major repairs. The structure of old buildings have outlived its life & needs to be pulled and developed afresh as further repairs to the existing structure won't be advisable & feasible. Redeveloped building have better magnitude life of reputed brands which entails low maintenance cost to the society
The Old Buildings are constructed according to old construction norms and hence cannot withstand with new climate and geographical conditions. New Buildings constructed according to latest building standards & amenties shall enable the member to have a pleasurable living Experience
Depending upon development rules & regulations, the members are also entitled to additional floor space or compensation in monetary terms. Capital appreciation in the sale value of member's flat is the biggest advantage in case of redevelopment projects. At times, it has been found that the capital apprecitation is approximately 3 times of the existing value of flats and the member gain immensely from redevelopment, Thus, redevelopment helps in unlocking the potential value of the member's flat which otherwise wouldn't have been possible.
Redeveloped building brings monetary benefits members of the society by way of payment towards corpus fund which in turn help them to better their lifestyle. Living in well developed building also adds to your social status and is often the envy of your friends and relatives.
