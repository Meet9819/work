About us

    Bhajiya Garam

In June’2013, created a company by the name STREAM FOOD WORKS Pvt. Ltd(SFWPL). Bhajiya Garam is the first Business Vertical of SFWPL.Brand name (Bhajiya Garam) :The word Biryani is derived from the persian word beryā(n) / Bhajiya Garam. It’s believed that biryani originated in Iran (modern day Persia).

In September 2013, the first Bhajiya Garam restaurant opened in Mumbai.

At first, it was an ordinary, if remarkably spotless, outlet. But as lunch time approached, the size of the crowd fascinated them, though the speed of service was an opportunity. They talked to customers, who were mostly families wanting to enjoy a wholesome meal together with their dear ones. They said they routinely drove out of their way to visit the outlet for the taste and variety offered.

It did not take much time to envisage a similar concept with hygienic, standardized processes; a limited menu; and good, inexpensive food in attractive modern packaging that is convenient to carry and consume for folks in a hurry. The guests would be all the more happy with a contemporary décor and warm employees with clean cut appearance and upbeat attitude.

Currently it operates seven outlets in Mumbai (Kandivali East, Borivali, Vile Parle, Kandivali- Charkop, Goregaon, Lower Parel and Mira Road).

Bhajiya Garam Mission Statement:

“Bhajiya Garam vision is to be the ‘Top of the Mind Biryani QSR’ in the market space that it operates in.

“Bhajiya Garam – is built on quality, service, cleanliness, and Value for spend (QSC&V), the very ideals, that are also blended meticulously in its operations.

Bhajiya Garam plans to become one of the renowned Quick-service Restaurant (QSR) company in the country by 2024.
